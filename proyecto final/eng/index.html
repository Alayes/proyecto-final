Our story

This blog started as a motivation that i had since i was a kid, to start my own web site about my own dishes and my specialities as a chef, all that i learn was thanks to my grandmother and from some tv shows.

My best dishes

Aqui encontraran recetas tipicas no solo de Argentina, tambien de España o de Italia. Les recomiendo probar mi paella, ya que, es la especialidad de la casa Here you can find not only the famous recipes from Argentina, i also like to cook traditional food from Spain and Italy. How this proyect started?

The idea to have my own blog started when i was 10 years old, i was always surfing on the internet looking for recipes and when i look to all the other blogs i get so excited to have my own one with my own ideas.
